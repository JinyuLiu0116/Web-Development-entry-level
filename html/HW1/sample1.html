The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now

On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist, and all the scientific journals her work had appeared, was a well_thumbed copy of On The Road.

She has spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote prolaiming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do.

Molly did think she had something better to do.
